About Vivid Sydney.

Vivid sydney is an annual festival of light, music and fun, lasting 23 days and featuring many of the world's most important creative industry forums, a mesmerising free public exhibition of outdoor light. Surrender to a symphony of taste, sight, sound and touch in a breathtaking two-hour journey through time and space – all while dining in a world-class, waterfront establishment. This immersive fine-dining event at 6HEAD in The Rocks begins with a Vivid Sydney-inspired welcome cocktail with stunning views of the harbour and the sparkly lights of Vivid Sydney and moves through a sublime dining experience to the accompaniment of theatrics, technology, lighting, storytelling and music. Presented by 6HEAD, SixthSense Agency and Red Balloon, the unique, dream-like digital and dining experience offers a choice of two seating sessions Monday through Sunday during Vivid Sydney 2022.

With so much to feel, see, touch, taste and hear during Vivid Sydney 2022, extending your experience with an indulgent city stay is a must-do. Sydney offers great accommodation options with special deals at Accor’s iconic Sofitel Sydney Wentworth, Mercure Sydney and Novotel City Central, all located at the centre of Vivid Sydney action, so you don’t have to miss a thing. Watch Sydney’s amazing transformation as the sun sets and explore a myriad of mind-blowing installations after dark across the city.
Spectacular Vivid Sydney...

“Watch Australia’s largest floating liquid and light show”

Want to be among the first to preview Sydney’s newest, superyacht-styled vessel ‘The Jackson’ before the official ‘opening’ in Spring 2022? Add to that invitation a sumptuous six-course degustation menu and fine Penfolds wine on the water, while you absorb the incredible sparkling harbour views. Named for the waters of Sydney Harbour (Port Jackson), the luxury $15 million vessel boasts elegant restaurant decks and a stunning cocktail bar, the perfect vantage points from which to connect with friends and experience all the colour and creativity of Vivid Sydney.
