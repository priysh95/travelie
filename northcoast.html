North Coast Road Trip!!

Let's take a tour to New South Wales Mid North Coast. We started our trip from Sydney towards the coast, to explore the nature at it's best. We left Sydney early morning to the New Castle, we reached Forster by afternoon. Then we headed to the Port Macquarie, we reached there by evening and then to our final destination - Coffs harbour. We reached to Coffs harbour around 8pm evening after a long awesome drive. We explored the resort that night and headed to our beds. Next day we woke up and headed to the crystal waterfall in the Dorrigo national park. Second day early morning we left for Muttonbird Island and the rainforest, we explored the muttonbird Island lookout and coffs harbour jetty. Next day, we visited Forest sky pier which was at 15m height and the Korora lookout filled with their cultural history and a 400m lush green trail that will take you to the scenic view that covers the coastline. Then the most awaiting day-weekend, On the first Saturday we went to Big banana fun park- a banana themed amusement park . On Sunday we flew back to Sydney.
